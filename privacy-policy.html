Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our services:

  • Personal Data: Your name, email address, phone number, payment details, and VIN.
  • Technical Data: Information about your device, IP address, browser type, and website usage behavior.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To process orders and deliver accurate and detailed VIN history reports.
  • To improve and optimize our website and services for a better user experience.
  • To ensure compliance with legal obligations and maintain operational security.

3. How We Protect Your Data

Your privacy is our priority, and we implement robust measures to safeguard your information:

  • All sensitive information, including payment details, is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Data is stored in secure systems, accessible only to authorized personnel under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Sharing Policy

We share your data only under the following circumstances:

  • With Trusted Third Parties: Limited data is shared with service providers for payment processing and report generation.
  • Legal Obligations: Data may be disclosed to comply with legal requirements or protect our rights.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Access and Correction: You can request access to or correction of your personal data at any time.
  • Data Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting us at Worldautosfacts@gmail.com.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience and monitor website performance. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or services. Updates will be posted on this page with the revision date clearly indicated.
